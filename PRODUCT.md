# ExtoArts

## Register
brand

## What it is
YouTube video editing and thumbnail design agency. Flat 10% fee model — clients pay editors directly, ExtoArts takes a small cut. Niche-matched editors (gaming, faceless, Shorts, motion graphics).

## Target users
YouTube creators who want retention-optimised edits and custom thumbnails without overpaying. Gaming creators (Minecraft, Roblox, PUBG, Free Fire) and faceless/automation channels.

## Brand personality
Confident. Dark. Technical energy. Not corporate — feels like a skilled team that gets gaming culture. Cyan/blue accent palette on near-black. Typography: Plus Jakarta Sans (self-hosted). Motion: cinematic, precise.

## Anti-references
Generic Fiverr-style freelancer sites. Wix template agencies. Anything warm/beige/sand.

## Stack
PHP 8.2 multi-page site. Plain HTML/CSS/JS. AOS for scroll reveals. GSAP + ScrollTrigger loaded. Self-hosted Plus Jakarta Sans. CSS custom properties for theming (dark default, light toggle).
